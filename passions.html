Passions

Architecture

I have always been fascinated by the role architecture plays in people's lives. The design of a physical space dictates the entire way in which we move on a daily basis.

Photography

How else to image a memory in a fresh way? Photography, to me, is a way to live through life embracing the joy and beauty in the simplist of forms.

Running

Running is a form of therapy, it clears the brain and rejuvinates the soul. I find peace in challenging my body because it keeps me yearning to grow and explore.

Jazz

Jazz is tranquility in a sound. There is something profound about jazz, it delivers a reaction that pushes us to move, to feel the beat and embrace the story beyond the notes.

Interior Design

Interior design is a way of bringing everything that feels right together and showcasing your personal style.

Tennis

Tennis is just fun, it teaches me to allow things to be playful, to find joy in competition, and build skills in something that brings me joy.
